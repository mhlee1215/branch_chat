Lead Generation Funnel

↶ ↷ ⧉

Start by identifying the audience's immediate problem, the language they use to describe it, and the channel where they already spend attention.

A lead magnet is something useful offered in exchange for contact information. It should solve one narrow problem quickly enough that a prospect wants it now.

Examples include ebooks, templates, discount codes, and free trials. For a small D2C hair care brand, each option should connect directly to a purchase path.

  • Ebooks: Create a short, actionable guide. For example, "10 DIY Hair Masks for Healthier Hair Using Kitchen Ingredients."
  • Templates: Provide ready-to-use resources. For example, a "Weekly Hair Care Routine Planner" template.
  • Discount Codes: Offer a percentage off the first purchase. For example, "Get 15% off your first order."
  • Free Trials: Let prospects experience the product. For example, "Try a travel-size shampoo and conditioner for free."
